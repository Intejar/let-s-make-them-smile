Our Donate Plan

On first view, one question will everyone think that how this your contribution can reach them directly. We have mainly three types of plan- Food, Shelter, Education. We focused on basic needs.Besides we do some camphaign every month.

Food for a family

Many unpreveleged people work hard or beg just to have a meal once in a day. But we waste so many foods. Inshort, your contribution can feed them once.

Shelter for a family

When we go outside, we see many beggars are sleeping on street. Our plan and your contribution can help them to sleep in a house.

Education for a child

Education is the pillar of success. We can not let people like these unpreveleged till their death. It is high time to make their child educated so that their future can turns into light.

Their lives depend on your donation
